Our story

Built on open ground.

Open Creative Studio was born from a simple idea: that learning shouldn't stop after school. We are a community hub where people of all ages come to explore art, design, music, cooking, nature, gaming, STEM, humanities and so much more.

The Open Ground is our first chapter — a season of planting, building, and discovering what this community needs. Each chapter ahead will carry its own name, its own focus, its own spirit.

We are open to every curious soul who walks through our door.
